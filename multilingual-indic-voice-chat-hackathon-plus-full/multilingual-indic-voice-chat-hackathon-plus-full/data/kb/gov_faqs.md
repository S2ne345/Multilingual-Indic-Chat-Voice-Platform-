
# Government Services FAQs (Sample KB)
How to apply for a ration card? Visit the state civil supplies department portal. Submit proof of residence and identity. Processing typically takes 15–30 days.
What documents are required for Aadhaar update? Valid ID proof, address proof, and biometric verification at an enrollment center.
How to check pension status? Use the e-pension portal with your ID; status updates appear within 48 hours after processing.
